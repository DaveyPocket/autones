Make your arduino a programmable NES/famicom controller.

Signal | Arduino | Famicom | NES
-------|---------|---------|----
Ground |  GND    | 1       | 1
Clock  |  D3     | 14      | 2
Latch  |  D2     | 12      | 3
Data	  |  A3     | 13      | 4


The NES Controller Port looks like this:

![](nes-controller-pinout.png)
